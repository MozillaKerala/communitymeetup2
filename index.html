The Meetup 2.0

We had the first Mozilla Kerala Meetup in September 2013 (Meetup 1.0). 18 passionate Mozillians from across Kerala attended the event. 18 strangers with a common dream - a better Web. We talked, inspired and got inspired. By the end of the day, the strangers were gone and a community was born. It has been a wonderful time for us - the community - since then. We did a lot of things, organized several events and took part in lots of activities. We taught little kids to code; we taught senior citizens how to use Internet safely; we introduced Firefox OS to developers; and what not!

So the time has come again for us to meet as a community, reflect on our activities since the last meetup and plan for the future. Our community has grown a lot more since the last meetup. More buzz is going on around and people have joined the community since then. In 2014, we have a lot more ground to cover and lot more people to reach. We have to build the Web that the world deserve and make sure everyone has equal opportunity to use and take part in building the Web. A web that is open for all, but still private for each.

Sounds interesting?

Then make sure you read and understand the FAQ section given below very carefully. And if you still believe you want to become a part of Mozilla Kerala, go ahead and Register.

About Mozilla Kerala

  • Our mission is to promote openness, innovation & opportunity on the Web. - Mozilla

    The Mozilla Kerala community is part of the global Mozilla community, primarily aimed at spreading the mission of Mozilla in Kerala. If you haven't guessed already, most of us here are hard core Mozilla nuts ☺ But like all geeks, we care about other stuff too :-)

    What unifies us as a community is that, all of us want to use technology for something good. Doing good is part of our code, duh.
