The Meetup 2.0

This is a small conference where a few mozillian meet to share the communitie's biggest hurdles and the future plans to make even the biggest challenges seem more possible. It’s where passionate thinkers and inventors come together to learn from one another and engage in a conversation about how the web can do more, and do better.

Regular Community meetup's strengthens the braintrust of bright people dedicated to championing a user-built web. This conference is filled with fiercely unconventional technologists and creators, eager to share their skills. So you too are invited to join us. Bring an idea and we’ll connect you with kindred spirits who will help nurture your ideas through hands-on sessions and interactive workshops. Find your community, hone your skills and amplify your voice around our common mission: ensuring the web is an innovation open to all.


Our motto is “less yack, more hack.”

We’re replacing darkened lecture halls with hands-on sessions and interactive workshops and there’ll be time to hack the ideas that emerge when hundreds of bright minds gather together in one space.

About Mozilla

  • Our mission is to promote openness, innovation & opportunity on the Web.

    At Mozilla, we’re a global community of technologists, thinkers and builders working together to keep the Internet alive and accessible, so people worldwide can be informed contributors and creators of the Web. We believe this act of human collaboration across an open platform is essential to individual growth and our collective future.

    Read the Mozilla Manifesto to learn even more about the values and principles that guide the pursuit of our mission.

  • Firefox

    We make Firefox with a mission to keep the power of the Web in the hands of users everywhere, We do more than that.

  • Join US

    The success of our mission depends on participation from people like you. Join us to build a better web.
